Consultancy

We offer consultancy services to our clients in analysing their current business system and system requirements for improved efficiency. In our evaluation we consider current work processors, other 3rd party software available, current technical capability and knowhow. Bileeta consultants will identify areas that need to be automated. Our consultants have vast experience across varied industry verticals and will work with our clients in finding the best suited solution for their organisation. They will offer advice and consider alternatives in each area, throughout the evaluation. The consultation will provide a Requirements Specification on which the total organisational requirements for the adoption of ERP would be included.

Post Implementation Support

An Enterprise Resource Planning system covers a vast area of programmed work and assists different types of operations, which may be spread across a large area or different locations. Managing such a system will at times require help from the system developers themselves. If you are a Bileeta customer, when you require help with Bileeta ERP, we provide you with customer support via phone, email and at times when required, our support team can visit your organisation. We believe in strengthening relationships with you so that you can get the most out of Bileeta ERP, as and when you need it. Our continuous maintenance and support assures our clients that they are in the best hands and that they have a partner in technology. Bileeta ERP clients can leave aside their worries and concentrate on their work and profit margins, we are there to help if the need arises.

ERP Awareness Programs

Bileeta conducts ERP awareness programs at organisations and institutions that can benefit from implementing the comprehensive ERP system designed by Bileeta. Specially targeting middle to large scale enterprises, the program would cover the following areas: What is an ERP system?

  • Overview of current industry adoption of ERP
  • Organisational readiness for implementation
  • A broad view of the Bileeta ERP
  • Benefits of implementing Bileeta ERP
